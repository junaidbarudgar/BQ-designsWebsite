ABOUT US

BQ Designs are a family-owned Interior fit-out and construction firm based in the Mumbai, India. Our dedicated in-house team provide comprehensive, full turnkey solutions for all interiors fit out and construction projects.

BQ Designs strives to become leaders in the industry of Interior fit out and construction. Through transparency and collaboration, we endeavor to create a customer centric culture resulting in repeat business, forging long lasting partnerships with all external stake holders who assist us in executing exceptional Interiors. Collaboration is key to the future of our industry and each member of our ever-growing team is committed to working tirelessly to achieve this goal. We aim to build a tangible legacy by assisting in the growth and development of the landscape in the Mumbai & Overseas soon.
